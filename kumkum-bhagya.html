Episode Summary

The episode begins with Ranbir putting his plan into motion to expose Rhea's manipulation and lies. He arranges a situation where Rhea would reveal her true intentions regarding her feelings for him and her hatred for Prachi. However, just as Rhea is about to expose herself, an unexpected visitor - Abhi - arrives at the Kohli mansion, causing the plan to fall apart.

Meanwhile, Prachi finds an old diary hidden in her mother Pragya's belongings. The diary contains revelations about her birth and a secret that Pragya has kept from her all these years. This discovery leaves Prachi stunned as she realizes that everything she believed about her past might be untrue.

At the same time, Pallavi finds herself in a difficult position when she overhears Rhea's true intentions. As Ranbir's mother, she wants to support her son's happiness, but she also feels obligated to maintain family harmony and uphold her principles. Her internal conflict becomes evident when she avoids confronting Rhea about what she heard.

Shahana, Prachi's cousin and confidante, teams up with Sid, who has recently discovered Rhea's manipulative nature. Together, they decide to gather evidence that would help reunite Ranbir and Prachi. However, they face opposition from Maya, who has her own reasons for wanting Ranbir and Prachi to remain separated. Maya strategically places obstacles in their path and misleads other family members about their intentions.

The episode reaches its climax when Prachi, still reeling from her discovery, comes face to face with Rhea in the Kohli mansion. The tension between them is palpable, and just as Prachi is about to confront Rhea with what she knows, the episode ends on a cliffhanger, leaving viewers eager to know what happens next.
