Episode Summary

The episode begins with Sejal gathering courage to speak to her family about her relationship with Rohan. Despite knowing that her traditional family might not approve of their inter-community marriage, she decides it's time to stand up for her love.

In a powerful scene, Sejal confronts her father Vikram, who initially reacts with anger and disappointment. The family argument escalates when her brother suggests that Rohan might only be interested in their family wealth. Sejal defends Rohan passionately, revealing how he has always supported her dreams and ambitions.

Meanwhile, Rohan faces his own challenges at home. His mother, who has always been supportive of his choices, surprisingly opposes his relationship with Sejal. In a shocking twist, she reveals a long-kept family secret - there was a bitter business rivalry between their families years ago that resulted in Rohan's father's downfall.

Arjun, who has always had feelings for Sejal, sees this as an opportunity to break them apart. He manipulates information and plants doubts in Sejal's mind about Rohan's true intentions. He suggests that Rohan might be seeking revenge for his family's past misfortunes.

The episode takes an emotional turn when Meera, Sejal's mother, unexpectedly supports her daughter. Having experienced the pain of being in a loveless arranged marriage herself, she doesn't want the same fate for Sejal. Her support creates a rift between her and Vikram.

The episode concludes with Vikram standing at a crossroads - should he prioritize his daughter's happiness or uphold family traditions and old grudges? His decision will have far-reaching consequences for everyone involved.
