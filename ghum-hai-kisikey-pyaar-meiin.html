Episode Summary

The episode begins with a frantic scene as Vinayak suddenly collapses during a family gathering. Virat immediately rushes to his son's side, with panic gripping the entire Chavan household. The child is burning with fever and appears to be having trouble breathing, causing alarm among everyone present.

Sai, being a doctor, quickly takes charge of the situation. Despite the tensions between her and the family, her medical instincts kick in as she examines Vinayak and determines he needs immediate medical attention. She recommends rushing him to the hospital rather than waiting for another doctor to arrive.

Virat, concerned only for his son's wellbeing, supports Sai's decision, which creates visible discomfort for Pakhi. As Virat and Sai work together to stabilize Vinayak before transport, Pakhi watches from a distance, her insecurities about their past relationship resurfacing. The concerned looks exchanged between Virat and Sai don't go unnoticed by Pakhi, who feels increasingly sidelined.

At the hospital, tensions escalate when Bhavani arrives and immediately blames Sai for Vinayak's condition, suggesting that problems only started when she re-entered their lives. Her accusations create a scene at the hospital, with other family members either supporting or opposing her views. Ashwini, who has always seen the good in Sai, stands up against Bhavani's accusations, causing a clear divide in the family.

Meanwhile, in a parallel storyline, Mohit and Karishma's relationship faces new challenges as they disagree about how to handle the current family crisis. Their argument reveals deeper issues in their marriage that have been brewing for some time.

The episode takes an unexpected turn when Shivani, while looking for medical documents for Vinayak, stumbles upon information about Patralekha's past that she had kept hidden from the family. This discovery promises to add another layer of complexity to the already tense family dynamics.

As the episode concludes, doctors confirm that Vinayak will recover, bringing relief to everyone. However, the emotional aftermath of the crisis leaves the relationships between Sai, Virat, and Pakhi more complicated than ever before.
