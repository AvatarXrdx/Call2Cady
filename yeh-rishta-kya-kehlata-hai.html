Episode Summary

The episode begins with Abhimanyu struggling with his emotions after discovering that Abhinav is raising his and Akshara's child. Meanwhile, Akshara is torn between her past with Abhimanyu and her present life with Abhinav, who has been a supportive partner and father figure to her son.

In a powerful scene, Abhimanyu confronts Akshara about keeping their child a secret from him for years. The emotional exchange reveals the deep wounds both characters still carry. Akshara explains her reasons, citing Abhimanyu's decision to choose his career over their relationship during a critical time.

The situation becomes more complicated when Abhinav reveals that he has known about the child's true parentage all along but chose to love and raise the boy as his own. This selfless admission moves everyone, including Abhimanyu, who begins to see Abhinav in a new light.

Meanwhile, Aarohi, who has had a complex relationship with her sister Akshara, makes a surprising decision to support her during this family crisis. Kairav stands firmly by Akshara's side, reminding everyone of the importance of family bonds.

The episode concludes with Manjari, Abhimanyu's mother, processing the news that she has a grandson she never knew about. Her conflicting emotions about Akshara's decision and her desire to be part of her grandson's life set the stage for future family dynamics.
